OUR MISSION STATEMENT

These pets that you viewed above were in desperate need of affection and care. Luckily, they were brought into our Homes For Best Friends shelter. We, the team at Home For Best Friends, are dedicated to helping vulnerable animals and making sure they are kept in safe, loving homes.

STATISTICS

With all of the services that shown above that we provide, we are able to save more pets each year. Take a look at our statistics for 2017.

The hard work that our employees, volunteers, and partners put in last year led us to save about 5.6 times more pets in December than we did in January. Last year, we also rescued 14,298 pets. And these numbers are already larger for 2018!
