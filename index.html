ABOUT US

These pets that you viewed above were in desperate need of affection and care. Luckily, they were brought into our Home For Best Friends shelter. We, the team at Home For Best Friends, are dedicated to helping vulnerable animals and making sure they are kept in safe, loving homes.

MEET OUR TEAM

Meet the people that make Home For Best Friends such an amazing animal shelter!

ALINA JOHNSON - FOUNDER

Alina always had a passion for pets. After finding a wounded dog in the street while walking home one day, Alina vowed to give stray pets a safe home. She opened up the Home For Best Friends Dog Shelter in 2010 with her best friend, Jasmine.

JASMINE CHEN - MANAGER

Jasmine, also a pet-lover, met Alina in high school. They always wanted to open a pet shelter together, and she wanted to become a leader as an adult. She found her dream job when Alina invited her to work at the Home For Best Friends Dog shelter.

JULIUS LUSTER - CARE TAKER

When Julius was a little boy, his family owned 10 cats and dogs. He always took care of each of them- he bathed them, fed them, and walked them. After seeing his love for animals, Alina swiftly made him an addition to the Home For Best Friends team in 2013.

ALEX SINGH - VETERINARIAN

Ever since he was a little boy, Alex always knew that he wanted to be a doctor. He went to medical school, but later realized that his medical training would be best applied to helping dogs. Thus, he joined the Home For Best Friends Team in 2015.
