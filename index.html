Alina Johnson - Founder

Alina always had a passion for dogs. After finding a wounded dog in the street while walking home one day, Alina vowed to always give dogs a safe and welcoming home. She opened up the Home For Best Friends Dog Shelter in 2010.

Volunteer at Home for Best Friends

Volunteers are the backbone of HFBF and without their time, talents, and resources we would not be able to do our daily operations and save the lives many animals each year.

  • Animal Care
    Volunteer at the shelter caring for the animals.This includes feeding, cleaning and walking dogs and cats.
  • Data Entry
    Volunteers will help with entering various pieces of information into our customized applications.
  • Front Desk Greeter
    Front Desk Coverage involves answering phones and greeting shelter guests
  • Fundraising & Friendraising
    Volunteers help with information tables, fundraising activities, and events.
