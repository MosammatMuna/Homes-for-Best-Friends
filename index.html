MEET OUR TEAM

Meet the people that made Home For Best Friends such an amazing dog shelter!

ALINA JOHNSON - FOUNDER

Alina always had a passion for pets. After finding a wounded dog in the street while walking home one day, Alina vowed to give stray pets a safe and welcoming home. She opened up the Home For Best Friends Dog Shelter in 2010 with her best friend, Jasmine.

JASMINE CHEN - MANAGER

Jasmine, also a pet-lover, met Alina in high school. They always talked about opening up a pet shelter together. Jasmine also wanted to become a leader as an adult. She found her dream job when Alina invited her to work at the Home For Best Friends Dog shelter.

JULIUS LUSTER - PET CARE TAKER

When Julius was a little boy, his family owned 10 pets. He always took care of each of them- he bathed them, fed them, and walked them. Seeing his love for animals, Alina swiftly made him an addition to the Home For Best Friend team in 2013.

ALEX SINGH - VETERINARIAN

Ever since he was a little boy, Alex always knew that he wanted to be a doctor. Thus, he went to medical school, but later realized that his medical training and skills would be best applied to helping dogs. Thus, he joined the Home For Best Friends Team in 2015.

VOLUNTEER AT HOME FOR BEST FRIENDS

Volunteers are the backbone of HFBF and without their time, talents, and resources we would not be able to do our daily operations and save the lives many animals each year.

  • Animal Care
    Volunteer at the shelter caring for the animals.This includes feeding, cleaning and walking dogs and cats.
  • Data Entry
    Volunteers will help with entering various pieces of information into our customized applications.
  • Front Desk Greeter
    Front Desk Coverage involves answering phones and greeting shelter guests
  • Fundraising & Friendraising
    Volunteers help with information tables, fundraising activities, and events.
  • Donate
    Donate to us so that we can continue to keep saving stray pets.
