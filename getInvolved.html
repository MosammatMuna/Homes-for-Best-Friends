TAKE CLASSES

Sign up to participate in classes or workshops to gain meaningful knowledge and experience in pet shelter services!

What are the benefits of these workshops?

  1. You receive free knowledge about how pet shelters function.
  2. Our hands-on teaching style teaches you how to do medical check-ups on our pets.
  3. After 30 hours of attending our workshops, you will be certified to work at HFBF.
