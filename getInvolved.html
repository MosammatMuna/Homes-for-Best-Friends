GET INVOLVED

Want to help? Here are three ways to become an active member of our community!

VOLUNTEER

Volunteers are the backbone of Home For Best Friends, and without their time, talents, and resources, we would not be able to perform our daily operations and save the lives of many pets.

  • Animal Care

    Volunteer at the shelter caring for the animals. This includes feeding, cleaning and walking dogs and cats.

  • Data Entry

    Volunteers will help with entering various pieces of information into our customized applications.

  • Front Desk Greeter

    Front Desk Coverage involves answering phones and greeting shelter guests.

  • Search and Rescue Team

    Join us in finding stray pets in the sreets and bringing them into our shelter.

TAKE CLASSES

Sign up to participate in classes or workshops to gain meaningful knowledge and experience in pet shelter services!

What are the benefits of these workshops?

  1. You receive free knowledge about how dog shelters function.

  2. Our hands-on teaching style teaches you how to do medical check-ups on our pets.

  3. After 30 hours of attending our workshops, you will be certified to work at HFBF.
